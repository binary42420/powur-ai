Please note that it may take up to 10 seconds for the response to process after uploading a document. Additional options are available by clicking the three dots to the right.

If you do not have a bill on hand, select a pre-uploaded example from the "Sample" dropdown. Some samples were intentionally uploaded in a blurry format to demonstrate the processor's flexibility.

It should be noted that Spanish was not included in the machine learning model's training. However, one of the samples is printed in Spanish to showcase the processor's adaptability.
- It should be noted that the SCE bill for Van was unable to identify the customer name field as a specialized entity due to the thin paper and the reverse side text bleeding through. However, upon uploading the full UB, the processor was able to correctly identify the customer name across various pages and verify their accuracy by matching them. If the complete bill had been uploaded, the processor would have successfully located and marked the customer name on the specific page. This issue could be resolved by providing additional training to the processor model.
- Entities fields are custom-trained specialized fields that extract specific key data points for automated entry into a database. It is intentional that the customer name is not labeled as a specialized entity. The confidence, not of the actually readability but of the form field and its location on the document, was under the configured threshold in order to prevent incorrect data from being stored into the final dataset.
- Although the customer name was in fact accurately read, the processor was not confident enough that what it read out was indeed the customer name, and so it decided not to label that field as one of these specialized entities.
- Due to this unlabeled field, this document would be forwarded to CS to for a manual human review before being sent back to the processor for further training of the machine learning model which would ultimately improve the machine learning models' understanding and lower similar future occurrences.
- Additionally, the meter number was not identified on the SDG&E Sample bill. This issue would be resolved by providing more training samples to the processor. The current training set comprises only a small number of samples for SDG&but an additional number of training documents would eliminate this issue.
- It is recommended that test documents be uploaded that include all pages to provide a more accurate representation. Despite the issues mentioned above, the processor still correctly extracted the information into searchable raw text form, just not with enough confidence to identify it as a desired entity.
- This can be verified on the "text" tab. This underscores the concept that once the processor receives additional training, it should have no problem extracting data from even less than desirable documents.
